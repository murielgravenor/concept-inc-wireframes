Event types we produce.

We've worked across every format of corporate event. Each has its own production logic — we know the difference.

Conferences & AGMs

Clear, consistent sound across large rooms. Confidence monitoring for speakers, broadcast-quality camera feeds, and an engineer who knows when to fade out and when to push levels.

Galas & award ceremonies

Atmospheric lighting that makes the room feel like an occasion. Stage lighting for presenters and recipients, LED backdrops, and sound that carries across a dinner layout without feedback.

Product launches

Production that makes a reveal feel like a moment. Precise cue timing, lighting that builds and breaks on brief, and a PA that handles both presenter audio and music playback cleanly.

Brand activations

Modular setups for environments that need to look considered, not plugged in. We understand the visual language of activations and spec accordingly — clean, branded, impactful.

Year-end functions

The full programme in one night — speeches, dinner, entertainment, and a dancefloor. We manage the transitions between each so nothing loses momentum or catches guests off guard.

Hybrid events

Live audiences and remote participants simultaneously. Camera feeds, stream encoding, confidence monitors, and an audio mix that works for the room and the recording — not just one or the other.

How we work with you.

A predictable process with no black boxes.

01 — Brief

Understand the event

Programme, venue, audience size, technical requirements, and the outcome you're briefing for. We ask the questions that prevent problems later.

02 — Proposal

Spec and price

A detailed production proposal — equipment list, crew, schedule, and clear all-in pricing. No surprises on invoice.

03 — Production

Load in and build

Full crew on site, on schedule. Rigging, cabling, system testing, and rehearsal support before doors open.

04 — Event day

Operate and resolve

Dedicated engineers running audio, lighting, and visual throughout. Whatever shifts on the day, we handle it without escalating to you.
