What's included.

Audio

  • Full PA system — ceremony, cocktail hour & reception
  • Digital mixing console (32-channel)
  • Wireless microphone — officiant channel
  • Wireless handheld — speeches & MC
  • Stage monitor / foldback for performers
  • Music playback & source management
  • Cable management & stage box

PA system is specified for the venue size. We walk the space beforehand to confirm placement and avoid coverage gaps or feedback risks.

Lighting

  • LED uplighting — up to 12 fixtures
  • Colour-matched to wedding palette
  • Ambient wash for reception space
  • Dancefloor effect lighting
  • DMX control system & programming
  • Ceremony ambient lighting (if required)

Colours are agreed during your pre-event consultation. We can match hex codes from florals or palette boards.

Service

  • Pre-event venue walk-through
  • Running order review with your coordinator
  • Full setup & sound check
  • 2 on-site technicians throughout
  • Live sound & lighting operation
  • On-call problem resolution
  • Professional packdown & venue sign-off

We liaise directly with your venue coordinator and planner — you don't need to manage that relationship on the day.

Perfect for.

The Signature is designed for receptions with a proper programme — seated dinner, speeches, and a dancefloor that runs into the night. At 80–100 guests, you need a PA that can carry background music during dinner and have real presence when the dancing starts. The uplighting count covers most mid-size venue perimeters.

It works particularly well for mixed indoor/outdoor setups: ceremony outside, reception in. The rig is sized and configured to serve both spaces without compromise. If your venue has a separate cocktail hour area, we'll configure for that too.

Most couples who book this tier have their own DJ or a live musician. We're set up to support both — the console and monitor system are ready to take an external DJ input or a live backline feed.

Good fit if you have…

  • 80–100 guests
  • Ceremony + reception (same or separate spaces)
  • A DJ or live musician
  • A dancefloor as part of the programme
  • Multiple speech moments
  • An indoor venue or covered outdoor
