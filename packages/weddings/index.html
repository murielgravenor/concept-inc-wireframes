Common questions.

Do you DJ?

We provide the sound system and can fully support a DJ you've booked separately — or recommend trusted DJs we've worked with across Cape Town and the Winelands. Full DJ packages are available on request. Talk to us during your consultation.

Do you provide microphones for speeches?

Yes. Every package includes at minimum one wireless microphone for your officiant and speeches. The Signature and Showcase tiers include a multi-channel wireless system, so your MC, officiant, and speech-givers all have dedicated mics.

What if there's a power issue on the day?

We carry backup power for all critical equipment on every job, and our technicians are trained to diagnose and resolve power problems without interrupting the event. We've worked every kind of venue, including those with unreliable supply. That contingency thinking is part of what you're paying for.

Can you work with our venue?

Yes. We've worked across the majority of Cape Town and Winelands venues and are comfortable assessing any new space before the event. Our pre-event consultation includes a venue review — whether in person or via plans and photos — so there are no surprises on the day.

Do you handle outdoor weddings?

Absolutely. We have weather-appropriate equipment for outdoor setups and have managed everything from clifftop ceremonies to winery lawn receptions. We plan for wind, sun, and contingency. Outdoor sound and lighting require a different approach to indoor — we've done both enough times to get it right.

When do you arrive on the day?

Typically 2–3 hours before your first guest arrival for Essential and Signature setups; 4–6 hours for Showcase builds. We agree a schedule with your coordinator or planner during the pre-event call and stick to it — we know you have enough logistics to manage without chasing the production team.

Not sure which package fits?

Every wedding is different. Tell us about your day — venue, guest count, style — and we'll recommend the right setup or build you a custom quote.
