Audio production

Sound that fills the room — and the moment.

We design and operate PA systems for any room, any audience size, and any event type. From intimate boardroom AV to outdoor festival line arrays, our engineers manage every signal chain — from source to speaker — so the client hears exactly what was promised in the brief.

Our approach is system-first: we spec the right rig for the space before anything is loaded in. That means no guesswork on the day, consistent gain structure, and mixes that translate across the room without dead spots or feedback surprises.

We also carry monitoring systems, radio microphones, and in-ear rigs — so performers, speakers, and MCs all have what they need to do their job confidently.

Lighting design

Lighting that shapes how a room feels.

Good lighting is invisible — it creates atmosphere, directs attention, and makes a space feel intentional without announcing itself. We design lighting for the event, not just the spec sheet. That means understanding the programme, the venue, the mood arc of the night, and how the room looks to every person in it.

From uplighting that transforms a blank venue into a brand moment, to moving heads and effect lighting that punctuate a performance, our rigs are designed, programmed, and operated by people who understand both theatre and live events.

We handle rigging, patching, DMX programming, and on-site operation. All equipment is owned and maintained in-house — no third-party sub-rentals with unknown history.

Visual & LED

Visual systems that command the room.

LED walls and video systems are increasingly a baseline expectation at corporate events, conferences, and high-end weddings. We supply, build, and operate LED panels at any scale — from a single presenter backdrop to multi-screen IMAG setups for large audiences.

We handle signal routing, video switching, and playback — as well as camera feeds for broadcast-style confidence monitoring, keynote presentation systems, and green room screens for backstage.

Projection mapping is a growth specialism for us and a strategic differentiator in the Cape Town market. We're actively developing our capability in mapped architectural projection and surface-specific content — for venue transformation, product launches, and immersive event formats that competitors can't readily replicate.

Full production

One brief. One team. One outcome.

Full production means we take the brief and own everything from there. Audio, lighting, visual, rigging, power, crew, logistics — managed as a single integrated system rather than a collection of separate suppliers who've never worked together before.

This matters more than most clients realise. When production is fragmented across multiple vendors, accountability gets fragmented too. When something shifts on the day — and something always shifts — nobody owns the problem. We do.

Our team has worked every side of live events: on stage, behind the desk, at the lighting console, in the rigging crew. We understand how a room feels and how it works. That dual perspective is what lets us design production that serves the experience, not just the technical spec.
