Before you ask

Common questions.

How far in advance should I book? ▾

As soon as you have a date. Popular weekends book six to twelve months out, particularly for weddings and large outdoor events. We sometimes have availability at shorter notice for weekday and off-peak dates — it's always worth asking. For bespoke and curated productions, lead time depends on the complexity of the brief.

Do you work outside Cape Town? ▾

Yes. We're based in Cape Town but regularly work in the Winelands, Garden Route, and nationally for larger productions and touring shows. Travel costs are quoted separately based on distance and crew size.

Can I get a quote before committing? ▾

Yes — all enquiries receive a full proposal at no cost and with no obligation to proceed. We'd rather quote accurately than give you a number that changes later. Send us your brief and we'll come back with a detailed spec and price.

What if my event is still in early planning? ▾

Send us what you have. A rough idea of date, venue, and guest count is enough to start the conversation. We'll ask the questions that help shape the rest — and we're used to working with briefs that evolve. Getting us involved early means fewer surprises later.
